Inspiration and Mission Statement

CAMA's inspiration according to the Boy(no one knew his real name)was the Camel and the Llama, He liked their trademark arrogant behaviour and their uniqueness compared to all the other animals. He loved the way that they both look weak at first but when they need to they can unleash deadly bites, kicks and the occasional spit. The boy couldn't choose from both so he named the Corporation CAMA, hybrid between is "I love Lamas and Camels."
